About Our Company!

Lights have become a necessity in the modern times. We cannot imagine our daily life without these. Where to find the best? Here at Abhil Group Of Technology. We are specialist when it comes to bringing forth innovative, highly efficient and flawless LED Lights, Floor Lights, Solar Lights, Street Lights, etc. They help millions nationwide with their durable ad reliable service. Daily hundreds of quantities are sold and we receive highly positive feedback from clients across the country. Along with offering a collection of uniquely designed Lights, we work as an ISO certified manufacturer and supplier of quality Poles. The Pole collection includes high quality MS Poles, GI Poles Octagonal Lighting Poles, High Mast Lighting Poles, High Mast Flag Poles, etc. These are constructed using premium metals, MS sheets, MS rods, aluminum casting and other quality materials. Further, top customization services are offered to clients for our offered range of Poles. Our Lighting Poles are used as highway and street lighting poles across the nation. The future goal of our company is to grow further and become the leading name in the pan Indian market.
